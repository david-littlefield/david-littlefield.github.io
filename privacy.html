SMS/Text Messaging

Consent: By creating a STŌR account or requesting a password reset code, you consent to receive SMS text messages containing one-time verification codes for account security and authentication purposes.

How We Use Your Phone Number: STŌR collects your phone number solely to send you one-time verification codes when you:

  • Create a new account (to verify phone number ownership)
  • Request a password reset (to verify your identity)

Message Frequency: You will receive one text message per verification request only. We do not send recurring, marketing, or promotional messages.

Message and Data Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier's plan.

Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Note that opting out will prevent you from receiving verification codes, which means you will not be able to create an account or reset your password via SMS.

Help: For help, reply HELP to any message or contact us at support@mystor.app

Privacy: We do not share, sell, or use your phone number for any purpose other than sending authentication codes that you explicitly request. Your phone number is securely stored and used only for account security.
